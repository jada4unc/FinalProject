Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredient List

10 Tablespoons (2/3 cup; 145g) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature

3/4 cup (150g) packed brown sugar

2/3 cup (160ml) unsulphured molasses

1 large egg, at room temperature

1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

3 and 1/2 cups (438g) all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 Tablespoonn ground ginger

1 Tablespoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground allspice

1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

optional: easy cookie icing or royal icing

Recipe:

Step One:

Beat butter for about a minute in a large bowl until completely smooth and creamy. Add the brown sugar and molasses and beat until combined. I prefer using a stand-mixer, but by hand works perfectly too! Next, beat in your egg and your vanilla (for about 3 minutes).

Step Two:

In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, allspice, and cloves together. Slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until thoroughly combined. The cookie dough should be a little sticky. You should chill the dough for a few hours!

Step Three:

Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). Line your baking sheets with parchment paper.

Step Four:

Remove chilled cookie dough from the refrigerator. Roll out your cookies until about 1/4-inch thick. Cut into fun shapes or use festive cookie cuters! Place your cookies 1 inch apart on your baking sheets.

Step Five:

Bake cookies for about 9-10 minutes. For soft gingerbread cookies, bake for 7-8 minutes.

Step Six:

Allow cookies to cool for 5 minutes on the cookie sheet. After the cookies are completely cool, decorate as you wish!

Step Seven:

The cookies stay fresh at room temperature for about a week!
